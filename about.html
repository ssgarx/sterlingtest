About us

At Sterling AG we like to think of ourselves as a group of relentlessly curious minds, conceptualisers, designers, consultants, and marketers who’ve picked the best bits of a large communication agency – and made it smaller. We believe it’s the future. Smart, flexible, cohesively structured communication experts who are personally vested in the success of your brand.

Here, each member of the team is given the freedom and responsibility to own their creative product. It’s what drives us to bring something new to the table everyday. So if you’re looking to work with us, prepare for an agile, innovative and most importantly, honest partnership.
